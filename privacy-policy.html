🧪 Privacy Policy

App: Cozy Cauldron
Developer: Wet Toast Studios
Contact: hvancefn@gmail.com
Effective date: April 17, 2025

Overview

Wet Toast Studios takes your privacy seriously. Cozy Cauldron does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. We have no servers. All game progress (gold, upgrades, prestige level) is saved locally on your device only.

Data we do NOT collect

  • No account registration or login
  • No name, email address, or personal identifiers
  • No location data
  • No camera or microphone access
  • No contact list or device files
  • No analytics from us personally

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Cozy Cauldron uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain data to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)
  • Approximate location (derived from IP)

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by Wet Toast Studios. We do not have access to it. You can review Google's privacy practices here:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized ads on Android via: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
On iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking

In-App Purchases

Cozy Cauldron offers a one-time "Remove Ads" purchase ($0.99) processed entirely through the Google Play Store or Apple App Store. We do not handle payment information directly. Please refer to Google's or Apple's privacy policies for purchase-related data handling.

Local Save Data

All game progress is stored locally on your device using your platform's standard app storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us. Uninstalling the app will delete this data.

Children

Cozy Cauldron is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. The advertising displayed via AdMob may be subject to additional restrictions depending on your jurisdiction.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how data is handled, we will update this page and revise the effective date above.
